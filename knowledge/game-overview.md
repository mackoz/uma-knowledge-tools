# Game Overview

*Last updated: 2026-07-11*

Uma Musume: Pretty Derby is a Cygames trainer/raising sim. You train "umamusume" (horse girls based on real Japanese racehorses) through a multi-year career of training turns and races, then use the finished trainees in PvP and events. The core loop:

1. **Career mode (training)** — build a trainee over ~3 in-game years using a deck of 6 support cards. The result is a permanent trainee with fixed stats/skills.
2. **PvP** — Team Trials (daily, async) and **Champions Meeting (CM)** (monthly tournament on a fixed course/conditions — the main endgame; most build planning targets it).
3. **Gacha** — two separate banners: characters (trainees) and support cards. Support cards are generally the bigger long-term power investment; SSRs want limit breaks (MLB = max limit break, 4 copies past the first).

## Global version

- Launched worldwide **June 26, 2025** (iOS/Android/Steam), roughly 4 years behind JP in content.
- Runs an **accelerated schedule**: banners and features arrive in mostly JP order but much faster (roughly 2–3× compressed).
- As of **July 2026**: the game is around its **1.5th Anniversary** (celebration missions run mid-July through August 2026). Recent/current banners: Gold Ship (Summer), Mejiro McQueen (Summer), Bamboo Memory.
- Launch scenario is **URA Finale**; later JP scenarios (Aoharu Hai Team Race, Grand Live, etc.) arrive on the accelerated schedule — **verify in-game which scenarios are currently available** before planning builds around one.
