## Why most of the list is a trap

Of ~35 purchasable skills, most can never proc for this uma/course and are worth 0 regardless of price:

- **Wrong style** (she's Pace Chaser): Fast-Paced, Restless+Moxie (Front Runner); Early Start, Standing By, Sleeping Lion, Intense Gaze (End Closer); A Small Breather (Late Surger).
- **Wrong distance** (1200m Sprint): Steadfast, Medium Straightaways ○ (Medium); Shifting Gears (Mile); Passing Pro (Long).
- **Wrong course/condition**: Left-Handed ○, Tokyo Racecourse ○, Fall/Winter Runner ○ (race is right-handed Nakayama, summer).
- **Zero on chart despite matching**: Standard Distance ◎ (0.00 — the passive's stat is already capped), Lucky Seven (0.00).
- **Recovery skills** (Corner/Straightaway Recovery, Preferred Position): unvalued by the chart *by design* (fork removes HP from it). Only worth buying if spurt rate is below ~100% — check the umalator spurt/survival rate before skipping. If stamina is already sufficient, they add nothing.
- **Debuffs** (Frenzied Pace Chasers, Flustered/Hesitant Front Runners): not valued by the chart; situational CM tools, unmeasured here.

## Caveats

- ΔL values assume additivity; combined sets aren't simulated together. Before confirming in-game, re-run umalator with all 4 skills added and compare vs current build.
- Mean ΔL hides variance — e.g. Turbo Sprint's proc is positional; Firm Conditions ◎ only pays off on firm ground (its 0.19 assumes the simmed condition).
- If this uma is for a CM on a different course/condition, the whole table changes — these values are Nakayama-1200m-specific.
